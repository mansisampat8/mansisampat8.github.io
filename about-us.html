Meet the Minds Behind Consumer Kanvas

Where bold ideas meet real consumer insight.

Mansi Sampat
Co-Founder, Research Strategist & Insight Partner
I’ve always believed that bold ideas deserve rigorous validation. With a career built at the intersection of law, literature, advertising, and consumer behavior, I bring a rare multi-disciplinary lens to business strategy.

After completing my Bachelor's in Mass Media (Advertising), I pursued a Law degree, followed by a Master’s in English Literature each layer refining how I read people, patterns, and the stories that move them. Five years in legal research at a leading LPO honed my investigative precision and cross-industry analysis.

But my true calling was always in understanding why people buy, what they believe, and how brands can speak to that truth.
