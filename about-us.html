Mansi Sampat
Co-Founder, Research Strategist & Insight Partner
I’ve always believed that bold ideas deserve rigorous validation. With a career built at the intersection of law, literature, advertising, and consumer behavior, I bring a rare multi-disciplinary lens to business strategy.

After completing my Bachelor's in Mass Media (Advertising), I pursued a Law degree, followed by a Master’s in English Literature each layer refining how I read people, patterns, and the stories that move them. Five years in legal research at a leading LPO honed my investigative precision and cross-industry analysis.

But my true calling was always in understanding why people buy, what they believe, and how brands can speak to that truth.

At Consumer Kanvas, I specialize in qualitative research, FGDs (focus group discussions), and IDIs (in-depth interviews) to uncover what most brands overlook: real consumer voice. Whether it’s a founder exploring product-market fit or a scaling brand refining their GTM, I help decode market signals into sharp strategy.

If you’re looking for a thinking partner who brings empathy, logic, and clarity to every business decision, you’re in the right place.

Nishita Mantry
Co-Founder, Brand Strategist & Mentor to 2500+ Women Entrepreneurs
Nishita Mantry is the force behind 300+ winning brands and the trusted strategist for over 500 women-led startups. As the Co-Founder of Consumer Kanvas, she blends brand building expertise with deep empathy for first-time founders.

Her mission?
To empower women to create businesses they love, and earn money they truly own.
As she says, “The taste of your own money is the sweetest.”

With over 2,500 women entrepreneurs trained and 1,000 business ideas mentored, she brings real-world insight, practical strategy, and heartfelt mentorship to every founder she works with.
