Mansi Sampat

I turn that into actionable growth strategies that save you time, money, and frustration. Think of it as your shortcut to sharper decisions and faster growth.

At Consumer Kanvas, I design and lead our Founder’s Edge Reports and Customer Conversion Labs; giving founders a clear map of their market, unfiltered customer truths, and a growth playbook that translates directly into sales.I don’t do jargon. I do clarity, action, and results.
